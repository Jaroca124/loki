Who is Loki?

Zachary Khan

Creator, Founder & iOS Developer

Zach is a self-taught iOS developer and entrepreneur who is motivated and passionate about what he does. In the beginning, Zach built the app for fun and to get better at iOS programming. After teaming with Jake and dedicating a lot of time towards the app, Loki has become something much more important than a fun project. Zach is 20 years old, and has a diverse list of interests including: Technology, Space, Sports, Auto/Moto Racing, entrepreneurship and of course Computer Programming.

Jacob Caccamo

Co-Founder, UI/UX Designer & Web Developer

Dedicated, passionate, and creative, Jake was the second man on the Loki team. He worked with Zach to build an elegant and cohesive experience. Jake is 20 years old and loves soccer, video games, and spending quality time hanging out with friends. He attends Tufts University in Boston earning a Bachelor's in Engineering Psychology (Human Factors) along with a Computer Science minor.
